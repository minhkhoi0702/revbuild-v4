About Us

Revbuild is an Australian managed business who collaborate with a highly qualified staffing team in Vietnam. Revbuild recognises the challenges the Australian construction and like industries are facing. Our key area of focus is labour. There is a substantial pipeline of work available within the construction and like industries, and yet productivity and profits are decreasing.

Revbuild offers a pathway for Australian companies to employ offshore reliable, affordable, more progressive, and qualified staff. Our services include estimating, architectural and engineering drafting, 3D rendering, graphic design, and general administration services.

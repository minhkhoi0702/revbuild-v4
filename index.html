What does Revbuild International do?

Our Revbuild International team currently provides services in new home in architecture drafting, land surveying, construction material take-off and estimating, cabinetry design, heating and cooling, 3D render and graphic design and other administrate services. All the above services comprise university qualified students along with experience in these areas.

The new reality is that we can work remotely from home, interstate and outsource overseas. The Revbuild international model means engaging a team member who is Soley dedicated (in most cases) directly to your organization.

Advantages of outsourcing:

Labour: we take the worry of the employment process, applications, interviews, demands and transitions ensuring short-term and long-term savings.

Domestic compliances including workers annual and statutory leave, compensation, superannuation, redundancy, payroll tax are real savings and immediate.

As suitably qualified as skilled employees demand higher salaries and benefits in a tight labour market, Revbuild can solve all these problems.

Recent surveyors suggest 52% of Australian staff will be looking for more money or for further business opportunities in the next 12 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is estimating?

Estimating is the process of calculating the cost of a construction project, including materials, labor, and other expenses.

Why do I need an estimator?

Estimators can help you accurately plan and budget your construction project, avoid cost overruns and delays, and ensure that you get the best value for your money.

What software do you use for estimating?

We use a variety of software, including Data Build, Build Xact, Excel spreadsheets, and Blue Beam, depending on the project's needs.
